 Program
   BeforeStart
     Gripper Activate
   Robot Program
     Call d02_home
     Call d02_amp_off
     Call d02_unplug
     Call d02_replug
     Call d02_amp_on
     Call d02_home
   d02_home
     Gripper Activate
     Gripper Move17mm (1)
     Gripper Close (1)
     MoveJ
       d02_home_p
   d02_amp_off
     Gripper Close (1)
     MoveL
       d02_amp_sw_p
       Zero FT Sensor
       Force Control
         Wait: 2.0
       d02_amp_sw_p
   d02_amp_on
     Gripper Close (1)
     MoveL
       d02_amp_sw_p
       Direction: Tool Z+
         Until (tool_contact_detection)
       Gripper Move12mm (1)
       Direction: Tool Z+
         Until (distance)
       Gripper Close (1)
       Force Control
         Wait: 2.0
         Gripper Move12mm (1)
       d02_amp_sw_p
   d02_unplug
     MoveL
       d02_plug_p
       Gripper Close (1)
       Direction: Tool Z+
         Until (tool_contact_detection)
           Gripper Move17mm (1)
           Direction: Tool Z+
             Until (distance)
           Gripper Close (1)
           MoveL
             Pull
         Until (distance)
           d02_plug_p
   d02_replug
     Insertion
       Zero FT Sensor
       Linear
         On error
           'Sequence if maximum distance is reached'
           Halt
       Gripper Move17mm (1)
       MoveL
         Direction: Tool Z-
           Until (distance)
